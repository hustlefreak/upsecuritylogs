# Security Guard System - User Manual

## Overview
The Security Guard System is a comprehensive attendance management solution designed for security personnel to manage staff and visitor logs efficiently. It features office management, attendance tracking, scheduling (calendar), and printable/exportable reports.

## 1. Getting Started
To access the system, open your web browser and navigate to the application URL provided by your administrator.
- **Login**: Enter your provided username and password.
- **Logout**: Click your profile picture/name in the top-right corner and select "Logout".

## 2. Dashboard
The Dashboard provides a real-time overview of current facility activity:
- **Active Staff**: Number of staff currently signed in.
- **Active Visitors**: Number of visitors currently on premises.
- **Recent Activity**: A chronological log of recent check-ins and check-outs.
- **Quick Actions**: Buttons to quickly log a new visitor or staff member.

## 3. Office Management
Manage the different departments or physical offices within your facility.
- **Add Office**: Navigate to "Offices" > "Add New". Enter the office name, location/floor, and contact person.
- **Edit Office**: Click the "Edit" button next to any office in the list to update its details.
- **Delete Office**: Click "Delete" to remove an office. (Note: Only allowed if no active staff/visitors are assigned to it).

## 4. Staff Attendance
Record and monitor when employees enter and leave the facility.
- **Check-in**: Navigate to "Staff Attendance". Search for the staff member by name or ID, and click "Check In".
- **Check-out**: Find the currently signed-in staff member and click "Check Out".
- **Manual Entry**: If a swipe card fails, you can manually enter the time by clicking "Manual Log" and selecting the date/time.

## 5. Visitor Management
Log and track guests, contractors, or interviewees.
- **New Visitor Form**: Go to "Visitors" > "New Visitor". Fill out:
  - Name and ID/License number
  - Who they are visiting (Select Staff or Office)
  - Purpose of visit
  - Time In (automatically set to current time)
- **Visitor Badges**: After saving, click "Print Badge" to generate a printable visitor badge.
- **Check-out Visitor**: Locate the visitor in the "Active Visitors" list and click "Check Out".

## 6. Calendar & Scheduling
View expected visitors and staff shifts in a calendar view.
- **Monthly/Weekly View**: Toggle between month or week view in the top right.
- **Expected Visitors**: Future visitor appointments appear on the calendar. Clicking an appointment shows details.
- **Add Appointment**: Click on any date to pre-register an upcoming visitor or event.

## 7. Reports & Exports
Generate detailed logs for auditing and payroll purposes.
- **Daily Attendance Report**: Go to "Reports" > "Daily Log". Select a date to view all activity for that day.
- **Filter by Office/Person**: Use the search filters at the top of the reports page to narrow results.
- **Export to Excel/CSV**: Click the "Export" button in the top right of any report table to download the data to your computer.
- **Print**: Click the "Print Report" button to generate a printer-friendly PDF layout.

## 8. Settings
Manage system configurations (requires Admin privileges).
- **Users**: Add or remove security guard accounts.
- **System Configuration**: Change facility name, time zone, and badge printing preferences.
